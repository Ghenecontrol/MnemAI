Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how MnemAI, Inc. ("MnemAI", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your data when you use our website, services, and industrial AI application (collectively, the "Service").

1. Data Collection

We collect information in two distinct contexts: marketing site interaction and integrated platform usage.

Marketing Site Data: When you request a demo or contact us, we collect your name, work email, company name, and any contextual message you provide. This is used solely to respond to your inquiry and scope potential pilot programs.

Platform & Operational Data: Once integrated into your plant environment, MnemAI collects technician-submitted maintenance notes (voice/text), work order context from your CMMS, and associated time-series sensor data (vibration, acoustics, temperature) from monitored asset classes.

2. AI & Federated Processing Architecture

Our fundamental architecture is designed to protect your operational IP. We utilize a Federated Learning model, which contractually guarantees:

  • Local Isolation: Your raw operational data, technician notes, and sensor telemetry never leave your isolated AWS environment and are never commingled with other clients' raw data.
  • Anonymized Pattern Extraction: Our models process your data locally to extract mathematical "failure fingerprints".
  • Pattern-Level Sharing: Only aggregated, heavily anonymized model weight updates—devoid of proprietary metadata, employee names, or specific machine serials—are transmitted to the global MnemAI network to improve matching confidence for all clients.

3. Data Retention

We retain your marketing contact information until you request deletion. Operational platform data is retained for the duration of our contract to maintain your local fingerprint library. Upon termination of service, all raw operational data and locally isolated models are securely destroyed within 30 days. However, the abstract pattern weights already contributed to the global federated model cannot be untangled or reversed into raw data, and thus remain part of the global model.

4. Security Practices

MnemAI implements strict, enterprise-grade security protocols, including:

  • Encryption in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256).
  • Strict IAM role isolation utilizing AWS S3 and KMS for per-client data boundaries.
  • Regular third-party penetration testing and compliance auditing tailored to industrial software standards.

5. Third-Party Subprocessors

To provide our Service, we utilize trusted cloud infrastructure providers. Our primary subprocessor is Amazon Web Services (AWS), providing IoT Core, Kinesis, Bedrock, SageMaker, and secure storage infrastructure. MnemAI does not sell your data to any third party.

6. User Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you or your employer may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data stored within our systems. Since MnemAI is a B2B service, requests regarding technician data should be routed through your Plant Manager or designated account administrator.
