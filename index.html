How It Works

A continuously compounding cycle that turns unstructured field notes into structured network intelligence.

Capture at the Edge

Technicians log low-friction voice notes, forms, or auto-suggested tags at the exact point of repair using a mobile app. No heavy typing required.

  • → Voice-to-text with industrial acoustic filtering
  • → Pulls sensor telemetry at the exact timestamp
  • → Works in offline/low-connectivity environments

Structure with AI

MnemAI uses specialized LLMs to extract a structured 'failure fingerprint' — Symptom → Cause → Fix — from messy, jargon-heavy technician language.

  • → Normalizes slang across different plants
  • → Eliminates tedious 15-field manual data entry
  • → Maps to ISO 14224 maintenance standards automatically

Real-time Matching

When new anomalies occur, they are checked against the structured fingerprint library. MnemAI surfaces confidence-ranked diagnostic suggestions to the active technician.

  • → Reduces Mean Time To Diagnose (MTTD)
  • → Surfaces past solutions for identical equipment classes
  • → Up-skills junior technicians immediately

Federated Compounding

Every resolved event makes the model smarter. Through Federated Learning, your entire plant network shares pattern intelligence without sharing raw data.

  • → Plant A's fix becomes Plant B's suggestion
  • → Raw proprietary data never leaves your environment
  • → The knowledge base scales with your operations

AI Technology & Infrastructure

Built on enterprise AWS architecture.

MnemAI leverages state-of-the-art multimodal extraction and time-series similarity models. Our infrastructure is designed for high-throughput sensor environments and strict data isolation.

Amazon Bedrock & LLMs

Powers our conversational interface and extraction engine. Transforms unstructured technician speech into rigid, searchable taxonomies (ISO 14224 compliant) instantly.

Amazon SageMaker

Runs our GPU-backed time-series anomaly detection. Matches current vibration, acoustic, and pressure signatures against historical 'failure fingerprints' to rank confidence.

IoT Core & Timestream

Ingests and stores high-throughput sensor telemetry. Syncs in real-time with existing CMMS platforms (MaintainX, Fiix) via AWS Lambda and API Gateway.

S3 & Strict Isolation

Client raw data is strictly isolated via IAM policies. The federated learning model shares only mathematical weights, never proprietary operational specifications.
